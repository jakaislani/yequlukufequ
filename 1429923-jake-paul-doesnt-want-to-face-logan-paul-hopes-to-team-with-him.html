Jake Paul Doesnt Want To Face Logan Paul, Hopes To Team With Him

Rather than facing WWE United States Champion Logan Paul, Jake Paul is interested in teaming up with his brother.

Logan Paul has become one of WWE’s top stars, as he beat Rey Mysterio to win the WWE United States Championship at WWE Crown Jewel. Jake Paul, a prominent boxer, has appeared a few times on WWE programming, leading some fans to buzz about a potential match.

After his fight with Andre August, Jake Paul was asked about a potential WrestleMania match against Logan Paul by MuscleManMalcolm.

“I’m trying to be on his team,” Jake Paul said. “I don’t want to go up against him. He’s a dog in WWE. I’m trying to be his tag team partner.”

In a recent interview, Jake highlighted Logan’s success in WWE.

Logan Paul is currently waiting for a new challenger to emerge through the WWE United States Championship Tournament, which will continue on the December 22 episode of WWE SmackDown. WrestleZone will have coverage of the show as it airs.
