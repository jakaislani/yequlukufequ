Boosie Badazz's Son Reveals Daily Earnings From &quot;Trapping&quot;

During a recent Instagram Live, Boosie Badazz chatted on the phone with his son Tootie Raww. Catching up, he asked the 20-year-old what he's up to these days, to which he replied, "trappin'." After hearing this, Boosie lets his son know that he doesn't have to go to those lengths just to earn some cash, as he's more than willing to help him out.

"I got a bag for you," he told Tootie. The up-and-coming rapper then revealed that he's doing pretty well for himself with his current ventures, earning around $15K per day. His father urged him to come home, insisting that he'd be trolling him online until he finally visited. "I miss him, so I'mma mess with him," he says.
