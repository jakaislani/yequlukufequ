Some Highlights of Amanda Thibault's Meteorology Career

Local 22/44 News chief meteorologist, Amanda started her career as a weather graphics producer for chief meteorologist, Denis Phillips at ABC Action News in 2007. Later, she joined WCTV Eyewitness News as a news intern to Mike McCall, Pinpoint Weather Center's chief meteorologist in 2011.
