Net Worth Of Cristian Castro

Often simply known as Cristian music style includes Latin pop, bolero, pop-rock, mariachi, Latin rock, and more. The famous Mexican pop singer, Cristian Castro, has an estimated net worth of $25 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

His genre is Latin pop/rock. He has been active in the music industry since the early 90s, and till today he has delivered his love and passion for music. His first album was Agua Nueva in 1992, and he was nominated for a Grammy for Best Latin Pop Album. He released Un Segundo en el Tiempo (1993), El Camino del Alma (1994), El Deseo de Oir Tu Voz (1996), Lo Mejor de Mi (1997), and Camino del Amor ( 1998).

He released his albums consistently, and after nearly a decade of hit singles, his album Mi Vida Sin Tu Amor (1999) was also nominated for Grammy Awards for Latin Music. His album Viva el Principe was released in 2010 and reached #1 on US Latin chart. He had released a tribute offer album Mi Tributo a Juan Gabriel in November of 2018, followed by a sold-out tour of Mexico and the US.

His recent work includes an album named Gracias Principe: El Mas Grande Tributo a Jose Jose, released in 2019. He had his singles chart more than 30 times, and his talents were proved to have smooth, airy tenor disciplined, excellent skills in traditional and classical music.

The Mexican singer Castro comes from a family of entertainers. His mother was an actress and singer, Veronica, and his father is reported to be comedian and actor Manuel Valdes. Castro made his debut performance at a young age alongside his mother in the telenovela El Derecho de  Nacer in 1981. Then he made Television appearances throughout a decade, acting in Jugemos a Cantar (1982), Herencia Maldita (1986), and Mi Segunda Madre (1989).

Cristian Castro Had Multiple Marriage

Cristian Castro has been married three times in his life. He has been in a relationship multiple times to date.

He was found deeply in love with a law student from Argentina named Valeria Liberman. Castro even took his mother to her graduation and within days had the courage to ask her hand for marriage. The couple was seen acting as newlyweds buying groceries at a supermarket during their time after the announcement of their engagement in Punta del Este, Uruguay. The couple suddenly went their own ways before their wedding dates.

Then Cristian met the Paraguayan model Gabriela Bo in December of 2002. They tied the knot at her parent’s house in Asuncion, Paraguay, just after three months of dating. As fast as they got married, they broke up at the same pace. Their marriage lasted for almost a year, and they got separated on 5 March 2004. The model had reported that he was unfaithful and abused her.

The Latin Pop singer seemed to have never stopped loving his ex-Jewish girlfriend, Valeria, as he got married to her in Key Biscayne, Florida, just after 18 days of his divorce with the Paraguayan model Bo.

On April 2005 Latin Billboard Awards in Miami, the lovebirds gave into their affection on the red carpet and showed their excitement for a new baby. The couple welcomed their first child on 16 June 2005.

The couple had everything going smoothly, and then their family conflicts were no secret. 

Valeria accompanied her husband Cristian to all the events looking all proud and happy, while he received great nominations and won the BMI Latin Music Award in Las Vegas with their princess daughter, Simone.

The pair was ready to grow their family from three to four as per their announcement made where they said that the new member was a boy. The couple looked very happy, and the singer showed affection by rubbing his wife’s growing belly at the Premios Juventud in Coral Gables, FL.

The Mexican singer shocked his fans when he announced that the couple had separated on 28 July 2009 and moving to Mexico in an exclusive interview with People Espanol on 9 August 2009.

Cristian Castro also dated Ingrid Irribaren in 2009. Then he dated Columbian Paola Erazo from 2010 to 2013 and had one daughter, Rafaella, together. In 2013 he was reported to be dating actress Ana Marquez, and after her, he dated Malillany Marin in the same year.

The Latin singer finally found a girlfriend to whom he could commit, and she was also from a musical background. Her name was Carolina Victoria Urban, and a violinist by profession. Castro expressed his admiration for his new girlfriend when he replied that “she was very happy and her family was great, the urban family is beautiful people, their history was of generations” when asked by reporters.

Cristian Castro Family Details

Cristian Castro’s mother, Veronica Castro, was clearly not satisfied with her son’s spontaneous marriage as she didn’t attend his wedding, and she had even shared with People Espanol that since her son’s marriage to Liberman.

The mother and son were no longer close back in 2005, and she hadn’t even met her granddaughter. She added that her relationship with Valeria was, “There are people you like and people you don’t like, people you can’t deal with, and I felt like she couldn’t stand me.”

Cristian has a half-brother on his mother’s side named Michel. From his marriages, he is a parent of three children. He has one daughter, Simone, and one son Mikhail with his ex-wife Valeria Liberman. He also has a daughter, Rafaela Amarine, from a former relationship with Paola Eraso.
