Destiny 2: All Crota's End Loot Table &amp; Drops

Recommended Videos

Crota’s End is making its way to Destiny 2, where players will have to work together to take down Crota, Oryx’s son, and the many trials awaiting them in this deadly encounter. It wouldn’t be a lethal encounter without any loot, and there will be multiple armor pieces and weapons appearing for everyone who completes each challenge.

Like previous raids, Crota’s End contains specific drops for each round. If you’re looking for particular armor and weapons, your best chance to earn everything is to return to this raid at least once a week, following the routine reset. This guide covers every weapon and armor drop that appears in Crota’s End loot tables in Destiny 2.

Every Armor & Weapon Drop in Crota’s End in Destiny 2

There are four encounters that yield loot for Destiny 2 players in Crota’s End. You have the Enter the Abyss, Cross the Bridge, Ir Yut the Deathsinger, and Crota, the final encounter. Of these four encounters, Crota is the only one that has a chance to drop the unique exotic for this raid, Necrochasm, an Auto Rifle. Players will also be working to earn the specific armor for the raid. It will be Willbreakers for Titans, the Unyielding set for Hunters, and Deathsinger’s for Warlocks. The weapons players should expect to loot are going to be the Fang of Ir Yut, Swordbreaker, Word of Crota, Abyss Defiant, Oversoul Edict, and Song of Ir Yut.
