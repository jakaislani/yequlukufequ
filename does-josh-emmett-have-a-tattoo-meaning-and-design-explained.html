Does Josh Emmett Have A Tattoo? Meaning And Design Explained

Josh Emmett is an MMA fighter. His tattoo has sparked interest among his admirers, with some attempting to fathom its meaning.

The professional mixed martial artist (MMA) Josh Emmett currently competes for the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) in the featherweight division. Emmett wrestled for 14 years, beginning in elementary school and ending in college.

Emmett has been a professional fighter since 2011. He has also competed in King of the Cage, and as of March 21, 2022, he is rated No. 7 in the UFC featherweight rankings. Moreover, Emmett is also an accomplished Brazilian jiu-jitsu grappler, having won a world championship in the no-gi blue belt division.

How Many Tattoos Does Josh Emmett Have?

People who know Josh know he is a tattoo fanatic, and he would eventually tattoo his whole sleeve. Looking at his pictures, it is evident that he has numerous tattoos on his body.

However, he has not specified exactly how many tattoos he has made. Judging by the pictures, the fighter has tattoos on his right chest, and his right arm is half-filled with colorful tattoos. 

Additionally, he has a Japanese letter symbol on his left chest right above his heart. He has a huge human skull with a world tour written in capslock covering his left-hand bicep. Moreover, there is a whole paragraph written that covers the whole left part of his ribs.

On his left and right side of the back, he has outlines of scary-looking clowns, similar to the character in the IT movie, Pennywise. 

Last, he has a small tattoo on both sides of his wrists, a V symbol, and round-shaped dynamite. Interestingly, he has not tatted on the lower part of his body. He has declared that he wants to cover his arms with tattoos. Thus, people will be seeing more of his body getting covered in the coming years.

He likes clown fish, the orange fish from the movie Finding Nemo, although he made the tattoo before the movie came out, and he even mentioned it was not Nemo and that it was brighter fifteen years ago. However, there is no information on other tattoos and their meanings.
