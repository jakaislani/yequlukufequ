Niecy Nash | Bio, Age, Net Worth, Relationship, Career

One of the most adaptable celebrities in the entertainment business is Niecy Nash. She has performed in stage plays and television shows as an actor, comedian, model, producer, and host.

In addition, Nash gained widespread recognition for her Emmy Award-winning work as the host of the Style Network program Clean House in 2010. For her role on the television series Reno 911! from 2003 to 2009, she even won two Primetime Emmy Awards for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Comedy Series.

1970 saw the birth of Niecy Nash in Palmdale, California. She is a Pisces and has her birthday on February 23rd. She is of African American and American nationality.

Where was Niecy Nash born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

Carol Denise Ensley was born as Niecy. She is the daughter of Margaret and Sonny Ensley. Niecy is the public face of M.A.V.I.S. (Mothers Against Violence in Schools), an organization her mother started back in 1993.

Following her younger brother’s terrible murder on his high school campus, the charity was created. The main goal of M.A.V.I.S. is to raise public awareness of the violence that kids experience on school grounds.

Niecy graduated from California State University, Dominquez Hills in Carson with a Bachelor of Arts degree.

How did Niecy Nash start her Professional Career?

Niecy was five years old when Lola Falana’s television appearances motivated her to pursue a career in acting. But in 1995, after making her feature film debut in Boys on the Sides, she started to fulfill her goal.

After that, she started making appearances in movies and TV series like Reb, E.R., Girlfriends, Cookie’s Fortune, and N.Y.P.D. Blue.

Her career finally took off when she was chosen to play T.T. and Deputy Raineesha Williams on Comic Central’s comedic series Reno 911! She became well-known as the host of the Style Network program Clean House as well.

Her roles in various TV series and movies, such as The Purpose, Trust Me, Guess Who, The Bachelor, Do Not Disturb, The Soul Man, Family Guy, Never Have I Ever, and A Million Little Things, helped her similarly win millions of hearts.
