Why is Missy Elliot Famous? How did Missy Elliot Lose Weight?

Missy Elliot is famous for being a rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer. She is the first female artist whose debut album debuted on the Billboard 200 charts.

How did Missy Elliot lose weight?

Missy Elliot once shared that she lost weight by taking out two major things from her diet; bread and any drink aside from water.

“Proudly to say it’s about 4 months I have only drank water no other juices or soda & I cut out bread & Lord knows that’s been the hardest for me! See I’ve NEVER been a water drinker but this has helped my skin. It really restore glow back & I only cut out JUNK FOOD. Sh*t that’s my weakness. #Facts but if I can give up those other things I’ll fight that to! Maybe two cupcakes a months,” she wrote in a social media post.

Missy Elliot illness

Is Missy Elliot ill? What is wrong with Missy Elliot? Missy Elliot shared in an interview in 2011 that she was living with Graves’ Disease, an autoimmune disease that affects the thyroid.
