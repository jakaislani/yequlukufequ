Wilke Itzin (Actor): Who is Gregory Itzins son?

Gregory Martin Itzin was an American character actor of film and television, perhaps best known for his role as Charles Logan in the action thriller series 24. Itzin, who was nominated for a Tony Award for his performance in 1993’s The Kentucky Cycle, joined the cast of 24 during the series’ fourth season in 2005.

Initially a recurring role as Vice President Charles Logan, Itzin returned the following season as President and became one of the show’s central figures. He was nominated for an Emmy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Drama Series for his performance.

Who is Gregory Itzin’s son?

Gregory is a married man. He married his wife Judie Itzin for more than 39 years and has two children. Their kids are son Wilke Itzin, an actor, and a daughter named Julia Itzin.
